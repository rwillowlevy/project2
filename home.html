Here's a little bit about us

Cherry Tree was established in 2020 revolving around the corona virus pandemic. We are here to provide you the information you need to stay up to date. Our goal is to give you easy access to Corona virus data. We display information from any date you apsire to from showcasing you the total number of cases, how many people tested positive with Corona, how many people came back negative with the virus, and total deaths. We are here to support you and help you during this rough time. Our way of giving back to the world is by displaying organizations, non-profits, and hopsitals that are in need of donations and assistance to get through this pandemic.

We hope this helps you out! If you're an organization that needs help with funding, head to our contact page to find out more on how to get displayed on our site! Thank you!
